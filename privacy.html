Privacy Policy for Drut PDF

Last updated: July 2026

Your privacy is extremely important to us. Drut PDF is designed with a privacy-first approach, ensuring that all document processing occurs locally on your device.

1. PDF Document Processing & Data Security

Drut PDF operates entirely locally on your device for all document operations, including reading, editing, merging, splitting, compressing, converting, locking, unlocking, and signing PDFs.

🔒 Complete On-Device Privacy: Your documents, files, images, and personal content are processed exclusively on your device. They are never uploaded, transmitted, or stored on any external servers or cloud infrastructure.

2. Local Application Data & User Controls

All application settings, tool preferences, bookmarks, and encrypted passwords are kept strictly inside your device's private application sandbox.

  • This data remains exclusively on your physical device and is never accessible to us.
  • Data Deletion: You can clear all locally stored application data at any time by navigating to Settings > Apps > Drut PDF > Storage > Clear Data on your device, or simply by uninstalling the application.

3. Storage & File Permissions

Drut PDF requests storage or file access permissions solely to enable you to browse, open, edit, and save PDF files on your device. This permission is used strictly for executing user-requested document actions and never for background collection or tracking.

4. Advertising & Third-Party Services (Google AdMob)

Drut PDF integrates Google AdMob to display advertisements within the app. To serve ads, prevent fraud, and deliver relevant content, Google AdMob may automatically collect and process certain non-personal device information.

Information collected by Google AdMob includes:

  • Advertising Identifiers (e.g., Android AAID)
  • IP address and coarse/approximate location data
  • Device specifications (model, manufacturer, operating system version)
  • Ad interactions, performance metrics, and diagnostic logs

For detailed information on how Google manages user data, please review the Google Privacy & Terms Policy.

Opting Out of Personalized Ads: You can manage or opt out of personalized interest-based advertising on your Android device by going to Settings > Google > Ads > Delete Advertising ID or Opt out of Ads Personalization.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Drut PDF does not knowingly direct advertisements to or collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EU/UK). If you believe a child has provided personal information through our advertising partner, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate remedial steps.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in app features or store compliance requirements. Any updates will be posted directly on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy or app permissions, feel free to reach out to us:

Privacy Policy

Your Privacy is Protected

  • No data collection or sharing
  • No analytics or tracking
  • All data stored locally

No Data Collection

Drut PDF does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app operates completely offline and does not require an internet connection. There are no analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind.

Local Data Storage

All bookmarks, favorites, and app preferences are stored exclusively on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to anyone except you. You have complete control and can delete this data at any time.

File Access

Drut PDF requires storage permission to read PDF files from your device. This access is used solely to display your documents and is never used to collect or transmit any information.

Password Storage

If you choose to save passwords for protected PDFs, they are stored locally using encrypted storage. Passwords never leave your device.

Drut PDF: 18-in-1 PDF Editor

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

🔒 Local Processing

Your PDF documents are edited locally and are never uploaded to any cloud server.

📢 Ad-Supported

Monetized through Google AdMob to keep core PDF editing features free.

🛡️ Encrypted Passwords

Protected PDF keys are stored safely using device-level local encryption.

1. PDF Document Processing & Data Storage

Drut PDF operates locally on your device for all PDF reading, editing, merging, splitting, and processing tasks. Your documents, files, and personal contents are processed entirely on-device and are never transmitted to or stored on outside servers.

2. Local Application Data & Controls

All app settings, bookmarks, tool preferences, and encrypted PDF passwords are stored strictly inside your device's private application sandbox.

  • This data remains exclusively on your device and is not accessible to us.
  • Data Deletion: You can wipe all local application data at any time by navigating to Settings > Apps > Drut PDF > Storage > Clear Data on your device, or by uninstalling the application.

3. Storage & File Permissions

Drut PDF requests file management or storage permissions solely to enable you to browse, open, modify, and save PDF files located on your storage drive. This permission is used strictly for user-requested document operations.

4. Advertising & Third-Party Services (Google AdMob)

Drut PDF uses Google AdMob to serve advertisements within the application. To deliver ads, prevent fraud, and serve relevant content, Google AdMob may collect and process certain device information automatically.

Information collected by Google AdMob includes:

  • Advertising ID (e.g., Android AAID)
  • IP address and approximate coarse location
  • Device model, manufacturer, and operating system version
  • Ad interaction and performance metrics

For more details on how Google processes user data, please review the Google Privacy & Terms Policy.

Opting Out of Personalized Advertising: You can manage or opt out of personalized interest-based ads on your Android device by going to Settings > Google > Ads > Delete Advertising ID / Opt out of Ads Personalization.

5. Children's Privacy

Drut PDF does not knowingly direct advertisements to or collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EU/UK). If you believe a child has provided personal information through our advertising partner, please contact us so we can take appropriate steps.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in app functionality or store compliance requirements. Updates will be published directly on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy or app permissions, feel free to reach out:

Email: support.drutin@gmail.com

Drut PDF: 18-in-1 PDF Editor

Privacy Policy

Your Privacy is Protected

  • No data collection or sharing
  • No analytics or tracking
  • All data stored locally

No Data Collection

Drut PDF does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app operates completely offline and does not require an internet connection. There are no analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind.

Local Data Storage

All bookmarks, favorites, and app preferences are stored exclusively on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to anyone except you. You have complete control and can delete this data at any time.

File Access

Drut PDF requires storage permission to read PDF files from your device. This access is used solely to display your documents and is never used to collect or transmit any information.

Password Storage

If you choose to save passwords for protected PDFs, they are stored locally using encrypted storage. Passwords never leave your device.

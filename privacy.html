1. PDF Processing & Security

Your privacy is extremely important to us. Drut PDF operates as an on-device utility suite designed for local document management.

Zero File Transmission: All PDF document reading, editing, merging, splitting, converting, signing, compressing, and text extraction tasks occur entirely on your local device. Your files are never uploaded to, processed on, or transmitted through external servers.

2. Local Application Storage & Data Controls

All app settings, tool preferences, bookmarks, and local application states are stored strictly within your device's private sandbox storage.

  • No Remote Storage: We do not host or store any of your document content or user profiles on cloud databases.
  • Data Deletion Instructions: You can completely erase all local application data at any time by navigating to Settings > Apps > Drut PDF > Storage > Clear Data on your Android device, or by uninstalling the application.

3. Device Storage & File Permissions

Drut PDF requests storage or file access permissions solely to enable core features requested directly by you—such as opening, editing, saving, and managing PDF files on your device's internal storage.

These permissions are strictly utilized for user-initiated file operations and are never used to access unrelated media or personal content.

4. Advertising & Third-Party Services (Google AdMob)

To support ongoing development and offer our utility features, Drut PDF integrates Google AdMob to display banner and native advertisements within the application.

While your document files remain strictly offline, Google AdMob may automatically collect and process certain non-personal device information to serve ads, prevent fraud, and measure ad performance:

  • Advertising Identifiers: Android Advertising ID (AAID)
  • Network & Location Data: IP address and approximate coarse location
  • Device Specifications: Device model, operating system version, and system language
  • App Performance Metrics: Ad interaction data, launch diagnostics, and crash logs

For detailed information on how Google collects and processes user data, please review the Google Privacy & Terms Policy.

5. Managing Personalized Ad Preferences

You have full control over interest-based advertising on your device. You can view, reset, or opt out of personalized ad targeting at any time via your Android device settings:

Opt-Out Path: Go to Settings > Google > Ads > Delete Advertising ID or Opt out of Ads Personalization.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA & GDPR Compliance)

Drut PDF is a productivity tool intended for students, professionals, and general audiences aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly direct targeted advertising to or collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the EU/UK).

If you believe a child has provided information through our third-party advertising partner, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate steps to address it.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically to reflect app functional enhancements or store compliance policy updates. Any updates will be published directly on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date at the top.
